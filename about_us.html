The Team


Colin Tulley, CEO

Colin is a member of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA). He holds an FI-CO certification from SAP and has over 10 years experience in delivering Financial systems with a focus on Business Planning and Consolidation. He has participated in multiple SAP implementations, upgrades and rollout projects in various industries such as Retail and Manufacturing. He holds strong analytical and problem-solving skills which are suitable for requirement gathering, process design, system development and implementation. His communication and analytical skills enable him to identify and meet business and technical needs by translating them into specific technical requirements in order to implement the appropriate application. His focus is growing IDS's product offering to include other data visualization tools such Tableau and Sap Analytics Cloud (SAC) and is developing a state of the art team to secure, lead and deliver new projects and missions.
