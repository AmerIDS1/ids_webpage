Odoo

What is Odoo ? Odoo is a unique, all-in-one management software. It's beautiful and easy-to-use.

Odoo in a Nutshell

Odoo is a suite of highly cost-effective open-source business applications (apps) which have been created for almost every need you can think of.
 
The Odoo suite of apps combine business operations management (accounting, HR, inventory, purchasing) with front office (CRM, sales, point-of-sale) and productivity tools (timesheets, chat), as well as e-commerce and marketing apps. What's more, all these apps are fully integrated with each other running on the same platform.
